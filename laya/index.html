About Our Mission

EcoScan was founded with a simple mission: to make plastic recycling easier, more accessible, and rewarding for everyone. We believe that small actions, when taken collectively, can lead to significant environmental impact.

Through our platform, we aim to:

  • Educate people about proper plastic recycling
  • Reduce plastic waste in landfills and oceans
  • Create incentives for sustainable behavior
  • Connect individuals with recycling facilities
  • Track and measure environmental impact

By partnering with recycling companies, environmental organizations, and local governments, we're building a comprehensive ecosystem that makes it easier and more rewarding to recycle plastic items.
